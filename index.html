Projects done by Android Club.

An app for VIT Chennai Freshers to guide them through their initial days at the college.

  • Freshers can Log in to the app using their unique Application number and department.
  • This application displays schedule for each college level and each departmental level orientation activities of VIT Chennai.
  • Proctor details are provided to every student through this app.
  • It also displays maps and directions for the various college buildings.
  • The app includes details (including contact details) of anti-ragging committee and student counsellors .
  • FAQs are provided to answer queries of freshers regarding hostels and FFCS .
